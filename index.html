Hobbies

What do I do in my spare time?

Rock Climbing

I've been climbing for about two years now, usually at a 5.10c/V4 level. I love to climb because it's physics in action - a true test of statics and dynamics! I usually prefer to boulder, but I occasionally top-rope when I get the time to make the trek outdoors.

Tennis

My Dad introduced me to tennis as a kid, and I've been playing ever since the age of 8. I generally prefer to play for fun, but I'll play the occasional competition or two.

Robots!

I've fallen in love with robots ever since I joined my FIRST Robotics Team, 3863, when I was a freshman in high school. I loved the competitive nature of the competition, and how it was essentially a meritocracy of who had the best engineered robot. Seeing teams like "The Cheesy Poofs" helped inspire me to become a Mechanical Engineer.

Gaming

Though I hardly have time for games anymore, I'll play the occasional role-playing game or MOBA just to decompress.
